Sack Race

Blind Cross

Engage in thrilling team-building at your campsite with a game of Blind Cross. Our guide introduces this exciting activity, promoting trust, communication, and coordination among participants. Elevate your camping experience with a unique blend of adventure and camaraderie through this memorable and interactive game.

Rope Mouth

Spice up your camping adventure with the entertaining and hilarious game of Rope Mouth. Our guide introduces this engaging activity, where participants attempt various challenges with a rope in their mouths, fostering laughter and friendly competition. Elevate your campsite atmosphere with this unique and amusing game that adds a touch of whimsy to outdoor bonding..

Wooden Skate

Boost teamwork and coordination with our Wooden Skate Partner Game. In this unique and engaging activity, participants work together using a wooden skate as a prop, navigating challenges and obstacles. Elevate your campsite experience with this fun and interactive game, fostering communication and camaraderie among participants as they skate through the adventure together.

Partner Game

Experience trust and teamwork with our Blindfolded Partner Walk game on an odd-even road. This engaging activity challenges pairs to navigate the path, enhancing communication and reliance on each other. Elevate your camping camaraderie with this unique blend of adventure and cooperation under the open sky.

Bowling

Turn your campsite into a fun bowling alley with our Water Bottle Bowling game. Kick a soccer ball to knock down strategically arranged water bottles, combining the excitement of soccer with the thrill of a classic bowling game. Elevate your camping experience with laughter, competition, and a refreshing take on traditional sports.
